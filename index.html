our cozy little café

Welcome to Brewbucks our cozy little café, me and my wife decided to open this cozy little place, which we call our home away from home. We agreed to open it at the tail end of 2005. We thought since it's a wild world out there, we would open a lovely cozy place to serve our delicious food and drinks.

We have made some renovations throughout the years, our goal was always keep the cozy café vibe. We felt like we want anyone who came in to feel right at home when they would visit us here at Brewbucks.

We cater for everyone, offering drinks that cater to both the tea and coffee lovers, and serving a variety of food that is both vegeterian and vegan friendly.

K.K slider & gulliver

We are incredibly proud to announce that we will be having the world renown artist K.K Slider performing at our cozy little café, along with the extremely talented Gulliver who will also have a regular slot.

K.K Slider will be performing on the third thursday of every month, he will be performing a vast modicum of his most famous songs, you can expect to hear classics such as Welcome New Horizons and Love Song amongst many others.

Gulliver will be performing a stand up routine every other monday, we guarantee that these are two acts you don't want to miss out on.
